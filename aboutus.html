Company History

Monsters Inc. was founded in 1933 by a visionary monster named James P. Sullivan, affectionately known as "Sully." The company began as a humble startup in Monstropolis, initially specializing in the business of scaring children to generate scream energy. Sully's dedication to perfection and his partnership with his best friend and fellow scarer, Mike Wazowski, quickly propelled the company to the forefront of the scream industry.

Over the years, Monsters Inc. became renowned for its unparalleled expertise in scaring and scream collection. They developed innovative scare technologies, trained top-notch scarers, and maintained a commitment to excellence that set them apart from the competition. For decades, their mission was to keep Monstropolis powered by screams, delivering reliable energy to every home and business.

However, in 2001, a pivotal moment in the company's history occurred. Sully and Mike discovered the power of laughter as a more sustainable and positive energy source. With a bold decision, they transformed Monsters Inc. from a scream factory into a laugh factory, forever changing the course of their company and Monstropolis.

The transition was challenging, but Monsters Inc. successfully reinvented itself as a leader in laugh collection and energy production. Today, Monsters Inc. stands as a beacon of laughter, innovation, and community engagement in Monstropolis, bringing joy and positivity to every corner of the city.

Mission Statement

At Monsters Inc., our mission is to bring laughter and happiness to the lives of monsters and humans alike while maintaining our commitment to energy efficiency, sustainability, and innovation.

Our Core Values

  • Laughter Above All: We believe that laughter is the most powerful and sustainable source of energy. We are dedicated to making the world a happier place through our laugh collection efforts.
  • Innovation: We continually strive to push the boundaries of technology and creativity, seeking new ways to collect laughs efficiently and responsibly.
  • Community Engagement: We are an integral part of the Monstropolis community. We actively engage with our fellow monsters and humans, fostering understanding, unity, and goodwill.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to environmental responsibility. Our laugh collection processes are designed to minimize our impact on the environment, ensuring a brighter future for Monstropolis.
  • Employee Satisfaction: Our employees are the heart and soul of our company. We prioritize their well-being, providing a safe, inclusive, and enjoyable workplace.
  • Ethical Practices: We conduct our business with the utmost integrity and ethical standards, fostering trust and respect within our organization and the wider community.
  • Continuous Improvement: We embrace change and strive for excellence in everything we do. We are constantly evolving to meet the evolving needs of our world.

With a rich history of excellence and a vision for a brighter, more joyful future, Monsters Inc. is dedicated to spreading laughter and positivity while leading the way in energy innovation. Together, we're making Monstropolis a better place for all.
