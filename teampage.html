Our Mission

At Monsters Inc., our success is driven by the dedication, talent, and creativity of our diverse team of monsters and humans. Get to know the leaders and staff who make our mission of spreading laughter and positivity a reality.

Leadership Team

James P. Sullivan (Sully)

Position: CEO and Founder

Bio: Sully, the visionary founder of Monsters Inc., has dedicated his life to bringing laughter to Monstropolis. With a heart of gold and a knack for leadership, he has guided our company through transitions and challenges, ensuring our commitment to positivity and innovation remains unwavering.

Mike Wazowski

Position: Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Bio: As Sully's right-hand monster, Mike is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations of Monsters Inc. His passion for efficiency, coupled with his humor, keeps our laugh collection and energy conversion processes running smoothly.

Celia Mae

Position: Director of Community Engagement

Bio: Celia Mae is the face of Monsters Inc. in the Monstropolis community. Her dedication to fostering goodwill between monsters and humans is unparalleled. She organizes community events, partnerships, and outreach programs that bring joy to everyone.

Roz

Position: Chief Administrative Officer (CAO)

Bio: Roz is the meticulous mastermind behind our administrative operations. She ensures that our paperwork, compliance, and ethics are always in check, even if her exterior demeanor suggests otherwise.

Employee Spotlight

Boo

Position: Lead Laughter Collector

Bio: Boo, the human girl who once found her way into our world, now plays a pivotal role in our laugh collection efforts. Her ability to connect with both humans and monsters makes her an invaluable asset to our team.

George Sanderson

Position: Lead Comedian

Bio: George has transitioned from a scare expert to a comedy virtuoso. His knack for making people laugh is unmatched, and he's become one of our star performers in the Laugh Factory.

Randall Boggs

Position: Environmental Sustainability Manager

Bio: Randall has found a new purpose in ensuring that our laugh collection and energy conversion processes are environmentally responsible. His dedication to sustainability has earned him the respect of our team and the community.
